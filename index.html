Wait what?

A blog about automation, continuous integration and other scary things I will explain in easy manner.

So what's going on here?



Hi there! My name is Alina and I created this blog because I remember
perfectly how overwhelmed I was when started my IT path many years ago.
Currently I'm an Automation Engineer and working hard to become a
Front-end developer.
I perfectly know how you feel no matter at what step you currently are.
When I started learning automation it seemed impossible to me.

I was insecure about my knowledge, my skills and even my ability to learn.
I'm not one of those lucky people who understand code instantly, have
sharp logic and looks like they were born coders.

I'm usual - just like you. And if I did it - you definitely can!
