About me


I was born in Ukraine in New Year's Eve in 1990 and I
Believe since that time I was dreaming to create some magic.
I moved to USA in 2014 having a Master's degree in
Management and some QA Analyst experience.

Attention to details and curious mind helped me being really
good at what I was doing. I have more than 4 years of
experience in Quality Assurance and currently I’m developing
myself as an Automation QA Engineer. I love coding but I love
QA too much to move to development.

Combination of those
desires makes me a perfect Automation who wants to develop
skills and cover all possible routine in testing life cycle.
Currently I’m working on automation coverage of SDK,
implementing TeamCity as a CI tool for my company
and working hard to develop my skills towards front-end
development.
